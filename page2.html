Diwali Traditions

Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is a festival celebrated by Hindus worldwide. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. Traditional Diwali celebrations include:

  • Decorating homes with diyas and rangoli
  • Exchanging gifts and sweets
  • Lighting firecrackers
  • Performing puja (prayers)

Festive Food

No celebration is complete without delicious food. Enjoy traditional Diwali sweets and snacks like ladoos, kheer, samosas, and more.
